## Source notes
- The main anchor document is the 2019 Labor Code.
- The 2012 and 1994 labor codes provide backward lineage for comparison.
- The 2020 and 2024 laws broaden the pilot with adjacent labor-market coverage.
- All documents come from the same official VBPL source family.

## Pilot constraints
- No uncontrolled document expansion.
- No non-official internet sources.
- No AI answer generation in this phase.
- Keep the set reviewable by engineering and legal review.

## Acceptance criteria
- Every entry has a stable VBPL item ID.
- Every entry resolves to a source page URL.
- Every entry can be fetched idempotently.
- Every entry either parses cleanly or produces a reviewable low-confidence result.
